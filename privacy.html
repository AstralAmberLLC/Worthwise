Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Worthwise is designed to help users understand and manage their personal finances. This Privacy Policy explains the basic information users should know before using the app.

Information You Provide

Worthwise may allow users to enter financial information such as transactions, accounts, balances, categories, notes, and related details.

How Information Is Used

Information entered into Worthwise is used to display summaries, charts, account information, transaction history, and other personal finance features inside the app.

Data Sharing

Worthwise does not sell your personal information. Any future third-party services used by the app will be disclosed here.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to support@worthwise.pro.
